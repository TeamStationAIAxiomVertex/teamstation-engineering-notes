## Engineering Note

Distributed engineering breaks when talent, security, delivery, and compliance live in separate systems. A US CTO or CIO does not need a louder vendor claim, they need a signal they can test inside the work loop.

The signal here is control plane + operating evidence + governance. TeamStation looks at this through role depth, review behavior, ownership, security, and delivery telemetry, bc the real risk is not finding ppl, it is trusting the wrong fit inside an AI-assisted system.

This TeamStation Distributed Engineering OS page is useful bc it explains the operating logic behind one control layer. Read it if you want the plain-English reason this matters before building distributed engineering teams across Latin America.
