## Engineering Note

CIO risk rises when engineering capacity grows faster than identity, device, compliance, and audit controls. A US CTO or CIO does not need a louder vendor claim, they need a signal they can test inside the work loop.

The signal here is CIO governance + vendor consolidation + audit evidence. TeamStation looks at this through role depth, review behavior, ownership, security, and delivery telemetry, bc the real risk is not finding ppl, it is trusting the wrong fit inside an AI-assisted system.

This TeamStation source page is useful bc it explains the operating logic behind cio governance signal. Read it if you want the plain-English reason this matters before building distributed engineering teams across Latin America.
