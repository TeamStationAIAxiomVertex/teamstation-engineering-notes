# Nearshore control plane for visibility across people, devices, and delivery

Date: 2026-07-21
Source: https://teamstation.dev/nearshore-control-plane

## Engineering Note

Most distributed engineering cost models end at the monthly invoice. The math goes sideways when access drift, unmanaged devices, slow reviews, ownership gaps, and rework live outside that number.

One control plane makes those costs visible. Who has access, which device is governed, where delivery is stuck, and who owns the next decision can sit in the same operating view, instead of being split across status calls and screenshots.

We built the Nearshore Control Plane page for the Series A CTO trying to model total engineering cost before coordination drag turns into burn. It maps people, devices, onboarding, compliance, cost, delivery, and accountability into one governed view, so the cost conversation includes the operating system around the code.
