## Engineering Note

Resume polish is weak proof when the work needs judgment, ownership, and clean feedback loops. A US CTO or CIO does not need prettier profiles, they need evidence that an engineer can hold the work under pressure.

The signal here is validation method + cognitive signal + work loop fit. TeamStation looks at this through Axiom Cortex, role depth, review behavior, ownership, security, and delivery telemetry, bc the real risk is not finding ppl, it is trusting the wrong fit inside an AI-assisted system.

This TeamStation Axiom Cortex page is useful bc it explains the operating logic behind engineer vetting before team placement. Read it if you want the plain-English reason evidence beats resume polish before building distributed engineering teams across Latin America.
