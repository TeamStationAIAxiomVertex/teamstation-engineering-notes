# QA automation as delivery telemetry, not test theater

Date: 2026-07-20
Source: https://teamstation.dev/hire/by-role/qa-automation-engineer

## Engineering Note

QA automation becomes theater when every script is green, but the release still depends on hope. Test count means very little when nobody can explain the missed edge case, the failure pattern, or who owns the quality decision.

TeamStation checks quality reasoning, edge case thinking, failure pattern detection, communication, and ownership before the engineer enters client work. Axiom Cortex keeps the interview video, answer evidence, mental model, process knowledge, clarity, cognitive load, and risk notes tied to the decision.

The QA Automation Engineer route exposes that proof chain, including Playwright, Cypress, and test strategy playbooks, B-Axiom scoring, and the client evidence console. That gives a CTO something concrete to inspect before automation becomes part of an AI-assisted delivery loop.
